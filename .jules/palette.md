## 2024-05-23 - Micro-UX: Standardize Button Styles
**Learning:** Hardcoded inline styles on buttons (e.g., 'background: #0FF') lead to poor accessibility and visual inconsistency. Replacing them with Tailwind utility classes and semantic components (Link, Icons) significantly improves UX and maintainability without adding new dependencies.
**Action:** Scan for inline styles on interactive elements and replace with theme-aware utility classes.
