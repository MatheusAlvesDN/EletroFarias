## 2026-03-18 - Accessible Form Validation in Login\n**Learning:** The login form in this app uses duplicated structures in `src/app/page.tsx` and `src/app/homepage/page.tsx`. Without explicit `role="alert"` and `aria-live="assertive"` on error containers, and `aria-invalid` paired with `aria-describedby` on inputs, screen readers fail to announce authentication errors effectively to users upon form submission failure.\n**Action:** Add `id="login-error"`, `role="alert"`, and `aria-live="assertive"` to error containers, and consistently link them to corresponding input fields using `aria-invalid={!!error}` and `aria-describedby={error ? "login-error" : undefined}` to ensure accessible feedback for asynchronous form validation across all duplicated forms.
