## 2023-10-27 - [Enhance login form accessibility and disabled states]
**Learning:** Adding explicit required indicators and ensuring disabled states apply to both inputs and form buttons reduces user confusion and prevents dual submissions. Utilizing `noValidate` lets custom validation flow cleanly without interference from browser defaults.
**Action:** Always add visually clear disabled states (including cursor and opacity changes) and explicit accessibility features (like `aria-live` for errors and `aria-hidden` for aesthetic requirement stars) when designing or modifying inputs and forms.
