## 2026-02-01 - Brand Color Contrast
**Learning:** Bright brand colors (like Cyan #0FF) often fail WCAG contrast ratios when used as backgrounds for white text. This makes buttons and links unreadable for users with visual impairments.
**Action:** When using bright brand colors for interactive elements, use a darker shade (e.g., cyan-700 instead of cyan-400) to ensure a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1, while maintaining brand identity through hover states or accents.
