## 2026-01-31 - [High Contrast for Brand Colors]
**Learning:** Bright brand colors (like Cyan #0FF) often fail accessibility contrast ratios when paired with white text (1.25:1 vs required 4.5:1). Darkening the shade to `cyan-700` preserves the hue family while fixing accessibility.
**Action:** When encountering bright primary buttons, verify contrast immediately and propose a darker shade from the same color palette.
