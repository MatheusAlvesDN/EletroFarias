## 2026-02-08 - Brand Color Contrast
**Learning:** The brand's primary cyan color (#00FFFF) has insufficient contrast (approx 1.25:1) when used as a background for white text, violating WCAG accessibility standards.
**Action:** Use a darker shade (e.g., `cyan-700` or `#0E7490`) for UI elements that require white text on a cyan background to ensure readability and compliance.
